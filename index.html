About Jade eSports

What is Jade?

Jade eSports is an eSports Team which was created by Magnus and his friends on 25th of September 2020. The major aim was to create a place where people could find players to play with and improve their gameplay and progress in their gaming career. JADE is an eSports organization. What is an eSports Organization? Well an eSports describes the world of competitive, organized video gaming. Competitors from different leagues or teams face off in the same games that are popular. Jade being an eSports team participates in this tournaments. The early 7 members of Jade were initially were part of a team called the '7 Deadly Sins'. But later the team grew and was renamed to Jade eSports. But, still the first 7 members are still attached to the previous name 7 deadly sins.
